Detailed Description

template<typename Shape_, int num_coords_, typename Coord_>
class FEAT::Geometry::PatchHaloSplitter< ConformalMesh< Shape_, num_coords_, Coord_ > >

Base-Mesh Patch Halo splitter implementation for ConformalMesh.

This class is used to split the base-mesh halos in the case of fully recursive partitioning to obtain the inter-patch halos of the child patches.

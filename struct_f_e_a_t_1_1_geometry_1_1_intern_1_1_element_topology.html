Detailed Description

template<typename Shape_, int dim_ = Shape_::dimension - 1>
struct FEAT::Geometry::Intern::ElementTopology< Shape_, dim_ >

Surrounding elements of a mesh element.

Stores references to the vertices/edges/faces that make up a mesh element. References to edges and faces are stored together with their orientations.

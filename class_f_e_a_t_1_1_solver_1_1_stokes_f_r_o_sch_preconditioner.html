template<typename Matrix_, typename Filter_>
class FEAT::Solver::StokesFROSchPreconditioner< Matrix_, Filter_ >

Trilinos StokesFROSchPreconditioner class template.

This class acts as a wrapper around the FROSch Preconditioner (block variant) from the Trilinos library.

Detailed Description

Grad(P)/Div(V) assembler class.

This class assembles the two bilinearforms \(\nabla p\) and \(\nabla\cdot v\) of the Stokes equations, from which the weak formulations are obtained by integration by parts, i.e.

\[ b(\psi,\varphi) := - \int_\Omega \psi \nabla \varphi\]

\[ d(\varphi,\psi) := - \int_\Omega (\nabla \cdot \varphi) \psi\]

Note
Both bilinearforms are by default multiplied by -1 to obtain symmetry of the resulting Stokes matrix.

Detailed Description

template<typename VectorVelo_, typename VectorPres_, typename SpaceVelo_, typename SpacePres_>
class FEAT::Assembly::TraceAssemblyStokesBodyForceAssemblyJob< VectorVelo_, VectorPres_, SpaceVelo_, SpacePres_ >

Assembly job for the body forces computation of a Stokes solution vector.

Template Parameters
VectorVelo_,VectorPres_The vector types of the velocity and pressure components, respectively.
SpaceVelo_,SpacePres_The finite element spaces for the velocity and pressure discretizations

This class implements the body force computation as proposed by Giles et al in [GLLS96]

Author
Peter Zajac

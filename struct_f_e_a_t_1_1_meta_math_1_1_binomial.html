template<int n_, int k_>
struct FEAT::MetaMath::Binomial< n_, k_ >

Binomial template meta-program.

This meta-program calculates the binomial coefficient

\[ {n \choose k} := \frac{n!}{k!(n-k)!} \]

at compile-time.
